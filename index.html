        How To Play

        1. During Reinforcement, click your own visible territories to place all available armies.
        2. In Order phase, click one of your territories, choose how many armies to send, then click an adjacent territory to queue a move or attack.
        3. Each territory may issue one outgoing order per turn. Click Resolve Turn to process every queued action in order.
        4. Forest entry removes 1 committed army, mountains boost defenders, and water blocks all movement.
        5. You only see your own lands and adjacent cells. Conquer every non-water territory to achieve Ascendancy.
