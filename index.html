        How To Play

        1. During Reinforcement, click your own visible territories to place all available armies.
        2. In Order phase, click one of your countries, choose how many armies to send, then click a bordering country to queue a move or attack.
        3. Each territory may issue one outgoing order per turn. Click Resolve Turn to process every queued action in order.
        4. Forest entry removes 1 committed army, mountains boost defenders, and oceans create impassable gaps between borders.
        5. You only see details around your holdings, but distant country silhouettes stay visible so you can read the world. Conquer every country to achieve Ascendancy.
